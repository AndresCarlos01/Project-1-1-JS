In My Zone

Sports

Sports hold a special place in my heart, representing some of my deepest passions throughout my life. From my earliest memories, sports have been an integral part of my existence, shaping my experiences and preferences. During my childhood, my primary focus was on two specific sports: soccer and football. Despite my interest in both, soccer became the sport I truly grew up playing and dedicating my time to. As a result, my love for soccer has become a defining aspect of my identity.

Beyond the thrill of competition, sports have taught me valuable life lessons such as discipline, resilience, and the importance of setting and achieving goals without being afraid to fail. Whether through casual games with friends or more formal competitions, sports have been a reliable source of joy, stress relief, and personal growth. My passion for sports extends beyond playing to include avidly following leagues, teams, and athletes, allowing me to stay connected to the ever-evolving world of sports. Just to list a few of the teams I support for soccer I am a fan of Barcelona (Spain) and Cruz Azul (Mexico) and for football I am a Dallas Cowboys fan.

Working Out

Working Out has been something that has become a hobby and passion of mine. As I kept growing up I reached a point where the demands of time made it challenging to continue playing sports, particularly soccer. Faced with the void left by the absence of sports, I found myself seeking an alternative to stay active and engaged. It was during this period that my best friend introduced me to the world of weightlifting and gym workouts. Intrigued by the idea, I accepted his invitation to join him one day, and that experience marked the beginning of a transformative journey.

The gym became more than a place for physical exercise; it became a sanctuary where I discovered a new and fulfilling hobby. The rhythm of lifting weights and the discipline of a workout routine not only filled the void left by soccer but also ignited a passion for maintaining a healthy and active lifestyle. Over time, working out has become an essential part of my daily routine, offering not just physical benefits but also mental well-being. The camaraderie built in the gym, the sense of achievement with each workout, and the continuous pursuit of personal fitness goals have collectively turned working out into a cornerstone of my identity. It's not just a pastime; it's a source of motivation, self-discovery, and enduring fulfillment.

Embracing the Gaming Universe

Gaming is a crucial part of the fabric of my life. It all began with my first video game console, a GameCube, and while I can't precisely recall the name of my first game, I vividly remember it being a Mario video game. This initial exposure marked the genesis of a passion that would captivate me for years to come. As I explored deeper into the realms of gaming, I found myself captivated by the diverse and immersive experiences it offered.

Throughout my life, when sports weren't claiming my attention, I could be found engrossed in video games for hours on end, of course with certain limitations imposed by my parents. The allure of virtual worlds, the challenge of conquering in-game obstacles, and the storytelling within video games became an integral part of my spare time. Navigating through various gaming platforms and genres, I developed a keen appreciation for the evolving landscape of the gaming industry. It wasn't merely a pastime; it became a means of exploration, relaxation, and a social connector, forging connections with friends over shared gaming experiences. Despite the parental restrictions, my enthusiasm for gaming persisted, growing alongside the industry's innovations, making it an enduring aspect of my identity and a cherished source of entertainment and creativity.
